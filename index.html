Snacking Chocolate

Each square of nib mor has less than 3 grams of sugar^ and is made to be shared. We start with organic dark chocolate made with 72% cacao and top each delicious square with crunchy, chewy, toasted cacao nibs, sea salt, mint, blueberries, cherries, and more. Available in snack bags and individually wrapped bars.

^ Or 7-8 grams of sugar in a serving of three squares. See nutritional information for total fat and saturated fat content.
